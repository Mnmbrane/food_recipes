Brown Sugar Glazed Carrots

Serves 20 as a side dish

Ingredients

  • 2.5 kg baby carrots, rinsed and patted dry
  • ½ cup brown sugar, loosely packed
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1½ tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F). Line two or three large sheet pans with aluminum foil or parchment paper for easy cleanup.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the melted butter, olive oil, brown sugar, minced garlic, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  3. Add the baby carrots to the bowl and toss until evenly coated with the glaze mixture.
  4. Spread the carrots in a single layer across the prepared sheet pans. Don't overcrowd them—the carrots need space to roast rather than steam.
  5. Roast for 25–35 minutes, stirring once halfway through and rotating the pans between racks for even cooking. The carrots are done when tender and caramelized at the edges.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Transfer to a serving dish and garnish with fresh chopped parsley if desired.

Tips

Pat the baby carrots dry before tossing with the glaze—this helps them caramelize better.
Baby carrots may cook a bit faster than cut carrots, so start checking around 20–25 minutes.
If making ahead, slightly undercook and reheat at 180°C (350°F) for 10 minutes before serving.
For extra richness, add a drizzle of honey or a splash of balsamic vinegar in the last 5 minutes of roasting.

Enjoy!
